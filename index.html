Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 15, 2025

CarCare ("we", "our application") takes the protection of your personal data very seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Data Collected

  • User account: automatically generated anonymous identifier (Firebase Authentication). No email address, name, or password is required to use the application.
  • Vehicle data: make, model, year, mileage, maintenance history — voluntarily entered by the user.
  • Fuel data: fuel volumes, prices, mileage — voluntarily entered by the user.
  • Preferences: language, currency — stored locally on the device.

2. Use of Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your reminders and maintenance history
  • Calculate your fuel consumption and expense statistics
  • Synchronize your data across your devices via Firebase
  • Send reminder notifications, with your permission

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Your data is processed through the following services:

  • Firebase (Google): secure data storage and authentication. Firebase Privacy Policy

4. Security

All data is transmitted via HTTPS and stored in Firebase Firestore, which is secured by strict access rules. Only the authenticated user can access their own data.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the application. You may request the deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us.

6. User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR and applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to the processing of your data

7. Children

CarCare is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy or to exercise your rights:

Email: privacy@carcare.app

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Any changes will be published on this page with a new date. We will notify you in the application in the event of a significant change.
